Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Aura Clips ("the Service") handles data. The Service is a private automation tool used by its owner to publish the owner's own videos to the owner's own connected accounts.

1. Information we access

When you authorize the Service via a platform's official OAuth (e.g. TikTok Login Kit, Google OAuth), we receive an access token and basic account identifiers (such as your open ID and display name via the user.info.basic scope) needed to confirm the authorized account and to publish videos to it.

2. How we use it

Tokens and identifiers are used solely to (a) verify the authorized account and (b) upload and publish the owner's own video content through the platform's Content Posting API. We do not use this data for advertising or profiling.

3. What we store

We store the OAuth access/refresh tokens securely on a private server only to maintain the authorized connection. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

4. Data retention & deletion

Generated video files are deleted from our server immediately after they are uploaded. Tokens are deleted when you revoke access or on request to the contact below.

5. Revoking access

You can revoke access anytime in your platform's connected-apps settings, which invalidates the stored tokens.
